Theorycraft

A form of applied game theory; the mathematical analysis of game mechanics to discover strategies and tactics that maximize player effectiveness. fields: statistics, operations research, modeling, analytical/combinatorial optimization, simulation.

Goals

Providing a framework which facilitates detailed analysis of optimal strategy, and resource allocation, in the context of today's popular games; and to provoke the reader to learn, participate, refine, and develop novel solutions.
